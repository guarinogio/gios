# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-31

GIOS is a reusable game engine and framework by azunain for building Android games.

This policy describes the privacy behavior of games built with GIOS.

## Data Collection

GIOS may support optional services such as analytics, advertising, remote configuration, crash reporting, and Google Play Games.

At this stage, GIOS stores consent preferences locally on the device.

## Consent

On first launch, the user can accept or reject optional data usage.

If consent is accepted, games may use analytics and advertising features.

If consent is rejected, games should not show personalized ads or collect analytics events.

## Local Storage

GIOS stores local save data, settings, and consent state on the device.

## Advertising

Future versions may integrate Google AdMob. Advertising behavior must respect the user's consent choice.

## Analytics

Future versions may integrate Firebase Analytics. Analytics behavior must respect the user's consent choice.

## Crash Reporting

Future versions may integrate crash reporting. Crash reporting behavior will be documented before release.
